Ruzo Tech Cookie Policy

Last updated: 27/12/2025

1) About this policy

Scope and standards

This policy explains how Ruzo Tech uses cookies and similar technologies on this website. It applies to visitors in the UK and EU and follows UK GDPR, EU GDPR, and PECR/ePrivacy consent requirements.

We only use non-essential/analytics cookies if you give consent. Strictly necessary cookies run because they are required for the site to function.

2) What cookies we use

Categories

Necessary (always on)

  • Used for site security, session stability, and remembering your cookie choices.

Analytics (requires consent)

  • Helps us measure traffic and improve the site. Disabled by default until you opt in.
  • When enabled, we may use Google Analytics. Data is collected and processed according to Google’s terms and your consent.

3) Your choices

Consent and control

You can choose:

  • Accept all — allow analytics (non-essential) cookies.
  • Reject non-essential — block analytics cookies; necessary cookies remain.
  • Manage preferences — toggle analytics on/off anytime.

Your selection is stored so we remember it on future visits. You can change your choice anytime below.

4) How to withdraw consent

Manage or disable cookies

  • Use the “Update cookie preferences” button above to switch analytics on or off.
  • You can also clear cookies in your browser settings to remove stored preferences.

When analytics are off, we do not load Google Analytics or place analytics cookies.

5) Changes

Updates to this policy

We may update this policy if our tools or practices change. The “Last updated” date shows the latest version.
