Monthly development packages

Ongoing service with hosting and domain handled.

We store your site data and include domain and hosting services. If you stop service, hosting ends and domain data is locked, but we preserve your site data. Handover is available per the policy below.

We manage domain handling and hosting maintenance. If you choose not to continue service, we preserve your site data; hosting ends and domain data is locked. You can request a handover of the site assets (code + in-project files). If monthly payments stop before reaching the cost of the one-time Brochure package, the remaining difference is due. If monthly payments exceed that cost, handover is free. Handover does not include databases or hosting environments; database connections and hosting are disabled unless you continue those features.

If you have database or saved data, you will receive a copy of all project data (including customer/user data within legal reason) upon handover. Expect 2–4 weeks for initial website development; if complications impact the timeline, we will contact you directly.
